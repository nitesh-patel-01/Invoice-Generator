FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InvoiceMaker Pro.

Is this invoice generator really free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no hidden charges, no watermarks on your invoices. We plan to keep it free forever.
Can I create GST invoices here?
Absolutely. You can add your GSTIN, client GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, and per-item tax percentages to generate fully GST-compliant invoices as per Indian GST rules.
Is my data safe?
All your invoice data is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is stored or sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device.
Can I download the invoice as PDF?
Yes. Click the "Download PDF" button to instantly save a high-quality PDF of your invoice. You can also use the Print option to save as PDF from your browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! InvoiceMaker Pro is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes including Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops.
Can I add multiple items and different tax rates?
Yes. You can add unlimited line items, each with its own description, quantity, rate, tax %, and discount % — ideal for mixed-rate GST invoices.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us at niteshpatel7479@gmail.com or call +91 79748 23298. We typically respond within 24 hours on working days.
Can I use this for international clients?
Yes! InvoiceMaker Pro supports multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, AED, JPY, and SGD — perfect for billing international clients.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InvoiceMaker Pro.

Is this invoice generator really free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no hidden charges, no watermarks on your invoices. We plan to keep it free forever.
Can I create GST invoices here?
Yes. Add your GSTIN, client GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, and per-item tax percentages for fully GST-compliant invoices.
Is my data safe?
All invoice data is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is stored or sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device.
Can I download the invoice as PDF?
Yes. Click "Download PDF" to instantly save a high-quality PDF. You can also use Print → Save as PDF from your browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive — works on Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops. The items form uses a mobile card layout for easy entry on small screens.
Can I add multiple items with different tax rates?
Yes. Add unlimited line items, each with its own description, quantity, rate, tax %, and discount %.
How do I contact support?
Call/WhatsApp: +91 79748 23298 | Email: niteshpatel7479@gmail.com. We respond within 24 hours on working days.
Can I use this for international clients?
Yes! Supports USD, EUR, GBP, AED, JPY, and SGD — perfect for billing international clients.

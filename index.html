Frequently Asked Questions

Is this invoice generator really free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no hidden charges, no watermarks on your invoices.
Can I create GST invoices here?
Absolutely. You can add your GSTIN, client GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, and per-item tax percentages to generate fully GST-compliant invoices.
Is my data safe?
All your invoice data is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is stored or sent to any server.
Can I download the invoice as PDF?
Yes. Click "Download PDF" button to instantly save a high-quality PDF of your invoice. You can also use Print to save as PDF.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! InvoiceMaker Pro is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes including Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops.
Can I add multiple items and different tax rates?
Yes. You can add unlimited line items, each with its own description, quantity, rate, tax %, and discount %.
